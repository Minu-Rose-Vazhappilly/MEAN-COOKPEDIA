Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Cuisine : Asian

Servings : 3

Mode of Cooking : Medium

Preparation Time

15 Minutes

Cooking Time

20 Minutes

Calorie Per Servings

250 kcal

Ingredients Needed
  1. Tofu, cubed
  2. Broccoli florets
  3. Carrots, sliced
  4. Bell peppers, sliced
  5. Soy sauce
  6. Ginger, minced
  7. Garlic, minced
  8. Sesame oil
  9. Cooked rice for serving
Cooking Instruction
  1. In a wok, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced ginger and garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add cubed tofu and stir-fry until golden brown.
  4. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the stir-fry and toss to combine.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.
